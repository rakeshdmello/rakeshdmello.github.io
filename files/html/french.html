The French Page

Why Learn French?

  • French is one of the most notable languages, alongside English. It is referred to as a romantic language and is spoken by the natives of France.
  • French have a colonial history. Just like England, they also ruled other countries and they speak French too. e.g. French is a main language in Canada. French influence can also be found in New Orleans in US.
  • Francophone is a French speaker.
  • Francophile is a lover of French.
  • Before you become a francophone, you need to be a Francophile.

Tips to Learn French

  • Do not over stress or overemphasize letters, words or syllables.
  • In French, each syllable of a word has just about equal stress. When speaking, try to pronounce each syllable of a word with equal emphasis.
  • Place slightly stronger emphasis on the last syllable of a group of words.
  • Speak smoothly, speak musically and speak evenly.
