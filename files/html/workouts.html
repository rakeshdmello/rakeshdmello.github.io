Plank - 30secs
  • This exercise is good for the spine and works the core.
  • Tighten the core and the gluteus.
  • Keep the body straight. Don't sink or raise your hips.
  • Breathe naturally.
  • It's not right if lower back or shoulders are sore.
  • You may feel abs burning or even shivering.
  • For too hard, lower your knees on the mat with ankles crossed in the air.
  • If easy, add weight on your lower back.
